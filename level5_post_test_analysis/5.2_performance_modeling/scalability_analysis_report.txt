VPN PROTOCOL SCALABILITY ANALYSIS
================================

Protocol: wireguard
  - Max Theoretical Connections: 500
  - Practical Limit (80% CPU): 80 connections
  - Throughput at Scale: 650 Mbps
  - Estimated Users (100Mbps/user): 650 users

Protocol: openvpn
  - Max Theoretical Connections: 100
  - Practical Limit (80% CPU): 25 connections
  - Throughput at Scale: 80 Mbps
  - Estimated Users (100Mbps/user): 80 users

Protocol: ipsec
  - Max Theoretical Connections: 200
  - Practical Limit (80% CPU): 45 connections
  - Throughput at Scale: 280 Mbps
  - Estimated Users (100Mbps/user): 280 users

SCALABILITY RANKING:
1. WireGuard - Excellent scalability, efficient resource usage
2. IPSec - Good scalability, moderate resource usage
3. OpenVPN - Limited scalability, high resource usage
